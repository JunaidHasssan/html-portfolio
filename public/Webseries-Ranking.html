The Best Web Series According To Junaid

Wanna Binge Watch? Give This List A Try!

Game of Thrones

8 Seasons - 73 Episodes


Game of Thrones is a masterfully crafted tale of power, loyalty, and deceit. With its intricate world-building and complex characters, this show will keep you on the edge of your seat. A must-watch for fans of fantasy and drama!

Money Heist

3 Seasons - 5 Parts - 41 Episodes


Money Heist is a masterclass in suspense and strategy. The Professor's clever plans will keep you hooked. A gripping, intense thrill ride!

Stranger Things

4 Seasons - 5th coming


Stranger Things is a nostalgic masterpiece that blends sci-fi, horror, and coming-of-age drama to perfection. With its lovable characters, eerie atmosphere, and thrilling plot twists, this show is a must-watch for anyone who loves mystery and adventure. Get ready to binge-watch and fall in love with Hawkins, Indiana!
